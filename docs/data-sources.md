# Data Sources

All datasets originate from the **Open Government Portal**.

## 1. Canadian Business Counts — With Employees
- Annual counts of active businesses with at least one employee.
- Used for:
  - New business creation trends
  - Provincial/territorial comparisons
  - COVID impact analysis

## 2. Canadian Business Counts — Without Employees
- Includes self‑employed individuals and contractors.
- Used for:
  - Parallel trend charts
  - Understanding micro‑business dynamics

## 3. Rural Canada Business Profile
- Contains:
  - Profitability indicators
  - Gross‑margin sectors
  - Revenue and expense averages
  - Rural vs. urban classifications

## 4. National Business Classification Dataset
- Large dataset with:
  - Sector-level breakdowns
  - City-level concentrations
  - Provincial comparisons
- Used for:
  - Sector insights
  - “Fun facts”
  - Pattern detection

## 5. Metadata Files
- Data dictionary
- Column definitions
- Transformation notes
- Rural/urban classification rules
