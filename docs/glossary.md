# Glossary

**Business Counts:**  
Annual dataset listing active businesses in Canada.

**With Employees:**  
Businesses with at least one paid employee.

**Without Employees:**  
Self‑employed individuals and contractors.

**Rural Indicator:**  
Classification based on Rural Canada Business Profile.

**NAICS:**  
North American Industry Classification System.

**Fun Fact:**  
A short insight combining data + contextual information.
