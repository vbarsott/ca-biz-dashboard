# KPI Definitions

## 1. New Business Creation

- **Definition:** Number of new active businesses per year.
- **Breakdowns:** Canada, provinces, territories.
- **Datasets:** Business Counts (with/without employees).

## 2. Growth Rate

- `(current_year - previous_year) / previous_year`

## 3. Profitability Rate

- `% of small businesses reporting positive net income`

## 4. Gross-Margin Extremes

- Highest and lowest margin sectors per province.

## 5. Rural vs. Urban Indicators

- Based on Rural Canada Business Profile dataset.

## 6. COVID Recovery Index

- Composite metric combining:
  - Business count rebound
  - Sector recovery
  - Regional resilience
