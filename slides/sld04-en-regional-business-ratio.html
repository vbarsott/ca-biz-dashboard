Business Employer to Self-Employed Ratio

As of December 2025, Nunavut is the only jurisdiction where employer businesses outnumber the self-employed (0.87×), a direct reflection of its government-centred economy and limited private sector activity. At the opposite end, Ontario (2.93×), British Columbia (2.77×), and Quebec (2.67×) each meet or exceed the national average.
