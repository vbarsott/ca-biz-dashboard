The Per-Capita Picture: BC Leads, Atlantic Canada Lags

Ontario has the most businesses in Canada in absolute terms — yet its per-capita density ranks 3rd. With over 16 million residents, its ~2 million businesses serve a much larger population pool, pulling the rate down to 125 per 1,000 people.
