Deep Dive
Canada's construction industry is worth ~$284 billion USD — yet nearly 1 in 4 construction workers is a solo operator with no employees at all.
That's over 255,000 one-person shops swinging hammers and signing invoices simultaneously. Add in firms with 1–4 staff and you account for over 80% of all construction businesses in Canada.
