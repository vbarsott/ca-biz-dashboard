Deep Dive
The solo courier and messenger business count nearly quadrupled from ~12,000 to 43,000 between 2015 and 2025 — a clear fingerprint of the rise of DoorDash, Uber Eats, Amazon Flex, and Instacart.
Meanwhile, Taxi & Rideshare self-employed businesses went from 26,700 to 67,300 — 2.5× more. The entry rate into transportation self-employment rose over 70% since 2014, driven almost entirely by app-based platforms.
